## The 5 W's

- **Who caused the incident?**  
  An organized group of unethical hackers known for targeting the healthcare and transportation industries.

- **What happened?**  
  Attackers gained access via phishing and deployed ransomware, encrypting critical medical records and demanding a ransom for the decryption key.

- **When did the incident occur?**  
  Tuesday morning at approximately 9:00 a.m.

- **Where did the incident happen?**  
  A small primary-care health clinic in the United States.

- **Why did the incident happen?**  
  The attackers' primary motive was financial gain (extortion), facilitated by a successful phishing attempt on clinic employees.

## Additional Notes

### Reflections & Questions

- How effective was the clinic's email filtering system?
- Were there offline backups available to restore data without paying the ransom?
- This incident highlights the critical need for regular employee **"Phishing Simulation"** training to prevent the initial point of entry.
